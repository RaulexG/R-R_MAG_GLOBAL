When Nature Strikes: Stories of Powerful Phenomena

by Raul · Published May 2025

Grammar focus: This article uses Past Continuous and Simple Past to describe natural events.

Nature can be calm and beautiful, but sometimes it shows its power in surprising ways. People around the world have experienced natural events that changed their lives. These stories tell us about moments when the Earth was moving, the sky was flashing, or the sea was rising.

In 2015, Hurricane Patricia hit Mexico. It was one of the strongest storms ever. While people were getting ready at home, the wind was blowing hard, and the rain was falling a lot. Trees were moving, and roofs were flying away. Many families were staying in shelters, hoping to be safe.

Another event happened in 1985 in Mexico City. An earthquake shook the ground early in the morning. While people were sleeping, buildings were falling down, and the streets were breaking. Rescue workers were looking for people, and neighbors were helping each other. It was a day of fear and courage.

Storms and earthquakes show us that the Earth is always changing. While we live our normal lives, nature can surprise us. It’s important to be ready and help each other when something bad happens. These stories show how strong nature is, and how strong people can be too.
