What Is the Men's Fashion Trend This Year?

by Ricardo · Published May 2025

Grammar focus: This article uses comparatives and superlatives to describe fashion.

This year, the trend is to break the norm, combining the unexpected with the familiar. Fashion reflects the times, and in 2025, men’s fashion focuses on versatility — clothes that are both sporty and formal. Here are some of the trendiest items you can wear:

1. Mini Shorts

Mini shorts are smaller than regular shorts. They make your legs look longer and are more suitable for summer. They are often combined with sandals and oversized shirts for a fresh, urban style.

2. Jackets

Jackets this year are more colorful than ever. Some have more pockets than last year’s models, while others are simpler but with brighter colors. The most stylish one was the summer blue Hermès jacket on the Milan runway.

3. Suits

Suits have become lighter and more comfortable. You can look more elegant without sacrificing comfort. The best men’s suit this year was presented by Dior.
