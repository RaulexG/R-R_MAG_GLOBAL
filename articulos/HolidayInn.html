by Ricardo · Published May 2025

Grammar focus: This article uses Present Perfect to describe experiences and improvements over time.

The Holiday Inn in Querétaro has become a popular hotel for many travelers because it has offered clean, comfortable rooms and good service. The hotel has helped guests enjoy their time in the city, and many of them have returned for more visits.

The rooms at the Holiday Inn Querétaro have provided comfort and quiet for many guests.
