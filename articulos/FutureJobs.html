Future Jobs: Careers of Tomorrow

by Ricardo · Published May 2025

Grammar focus: This article uses going to to talk about future jobs and careers.

According to a study conducted by the World Economic Forum, jobs with exponential growth in the coming years were identified. The survey of more than 800 companies identified the following 5 jobs of the future:

1. Artificial Intelligence Specialist

The objective of this job is to simulate human thinking through computer systems to solve complex problems. These systems will complete activities that humans commonly perform.
HP is going to implement an AI system next year to produce printers in less time.

2. Environmental Sustainability Engineer

This specialty is going to manage projects to reduce polluting emissions, reduce energy consumption, and is going to work in the development of environmental policies in accordance with current regulations.

3. Business Intelligence Analyst (BI)

A business intelligence analyst is going to study data sets to help companies make business decisions.
By processing a vast amount of information, the analyst is going to identify vulnerabilities and propose changes to improve the company's efficiency and productivity.

4. Robotics Engineer

Helps create robotic systems used to perform human and non-human tasks. This engineer is going to design prototype robotic systems, build, maintain, and repair the machines, and is going to do research to develop new applications for existing robots.

5. Information Security Analyst

Information security analysts are going to protect computer networks, systems, databases, and any type of sensitive information from cyberattacks.
To achieve this, the professional is going to work as part of a team, designing, adapting, monitoring, and updating defense systems and responding to attacks.

Experts project a sharp acceleration in the emergence of new job opportunities.
