A Healthy Life

by Ricardo · Published May 2025

Grammar focus: This article uses should to give advice on healthy habits.

A healthy life means doing good things for your body. These are called healthy habits. If you want to start living a healthy life, here are some useful tips:

  • You should choose one healthy habit.
  • You should set a time to reach your goal.
  • You should have other ideas if one doesn’t work.
  • You should put reminders around you.
  • You should mark the days you follow your habit on a calendar.

To Live Better

  • You shouldn't smoke.
  • You should do exercise for 30 minutes.
  • You should read for 10 minutes to feel calm.
  • You should carry a water bottle to drink more water.
  • You should sleep 8 hours to rest well.
